To Divija. In the language of the stars, your name means Divine—born of the heavens. Yet, on Earth, you wield logic like a scalpel. Your skill in mathematics is not just talent; it is the way you structure your universe. You are a "tough cookie," armored in independence, grinding with a work ethic that outlasts the sun.

I will be honest. When we first met, our paths did not align in harmony. I was pissed. I saw rudeness, immaturity, and a sharp tongue that cut without remorse. I considered you hopeless. But mathematics teaches us to look for the variable that changes the equation. I stayed. I looked past the initial static.

And there it was. A gem hidden behind layers of defense. I realized your sharpness was a shield for a sensitivity you rarely show. I watched notes pile up—every one a reflection of how you captivated me, despite the odds. You went from a rude stranger to my constant thought.

Today marks a milestone. You are not just the tough girl I met; you have reached a point of true maturity. You have evolved into someone I can rest my heart upon. You have changed for the good, Divija, finally seeing the worth that I saw from the start.

We have shared memories, played through the friction, and built a year that means more to me than you know. You are amazing. You are divine.

I love you.
